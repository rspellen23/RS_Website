About

Learning Experience Designer. Creative Strategist.

I've spent the better part of a decade working at the intersection of instructional design, visual craft, and emerging technology. My work shows up in Fortune 500 onboarding programs, custom eLearning builds, and leadership development curricula — anywhere complexity needs to be made clear.

I'm drawn to projects where the brief is hard. The ones where you have to actually understand the domain before you can design the experience. That's where the interesting work lives.

Outside of client work, I'm exploring how AI tools are changing what's possible in rapid prototyping, personalization, and adaptive learning design.
